DLTK documentation
==================

DLTK is a neural networks toolkit written in python, on top of `TensorFlow <https://github.com/tensorflow/tensorflow>`_. It is developed to enable fast prototyping with a low entry threshold and ensure reproducibility in image analysis applications, with a particular focus on medical imaging. Its goal is  to provide the community with state of the art methods and models and to accelerate research in this exciting field.


Getting started
---------------

For installation instructions, getting started, contributing, etc. please refer to the `README.md <https://github.com/DLTK/DLTK/tree/master>`_.
